# shop-assistant-ai
Proposal: Shop Assistant AI
​Our Goal: To digitize your shop and provide 24/7 service without the need for extra staff.
​1. What can this app do now? (Current Version)
​24/7 Customer Support: Customers can chat at any time to ask about shop hours, product availability, or general information.
​Hinglish Conversations: The AI communicates naturally in "Hinglish" (a mix of Hindi and English), making interactions warm and relatable for your customers.
​Quick Reply Buttons: Four pre-configured buttons are available for the most common questions, making it easy for customers to get answers without typing.
​Safety Guardrails: It includes Google's built-in safety system that automatically blocks harmful, offensive, or off-topic content.
​2. Upcoming Features
​We are continuously updating the app, and the following features will be available soon:
​Product Catalog: You will be able to upload your entire product list with photos so customers can browse your items directly.
​Order Management: Customers will be able to place orders directly through the chat interface.
​WhatsApp Integration: The same smart AI assistant will be integrated to work directly within your WhatsApp.
​A brief pitch for you to use with shop owners:
​"Think of this as a digital counter for your shop. As soon as a customer asks about your business, this AI will provide an instant response, helping you increase sales while saving you valuable time."
